Description

Victor and Konrad are the twin brothers in the Frankenstein family, they share an unbreakable bond. Throughout their childhood, they embark on countless imaginary adventures, until their playful fantasies become a reality. One day, while exploring the family's estate, they stumble upon the Dark Library, a secret room filled with ancient tomes on alchemy and remedies. Despite their father's warnings, they can't resist the allure of the forbidden knowledge. However, their curiosity comes at a cost when Konrad falls deathly ill. Victor is convinced that the only way to save his brother is to create the Elixir of Life, an ancient formula he discovered in the Dark Library. With the help of his cousin Elizabeth and their friend Henry, they set out to find a renowned alchemist who can assist them in their quest. The three friends embark on a perilous journey, risking their lives to collect the rare and dangerous ingredients needed for the elixir. In their desperate pursuit to save Konrad, they face challenges that push them to the brink of their physical and emotional limits. But their struggles are far from over. As they draw closer to completing the elixir, they find themselves entangled in a web of illicit love and dangerous alliances that threaten to tear them apart. In this thrilling tale of adventure, sacrifice, and love, the three friends must navigate treacherous waters to save Konrad's life and preserve their bond as brothers. - Anav
